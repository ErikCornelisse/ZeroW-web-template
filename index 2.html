About the Chorizo project

The Chorizo Project Food Loss and Waste (FLW) Datahub and “Insighter” serves as a central platform for storing and sharing a wide range of datasets and insights on food loss and waste (FLW) across the EU. This hub features an evidence-based analysis on both previous and ongoing FLW initiatives across the EU, alongside new research on social norms and behaviours. These insights were gathered from several case studies conducted as part of the CHORIZO project, which examined the actions of consumers, businesses, and charities (e.g. food banks) in relation to FLW. Users can access and download datasets upon registration.

About ZeroW

ZeroW addresses the need to move from today's fragmented solutions targeted on isolated food loss and waste (FLW) problems to synergised solutions based on shared data, knowledge, and collective intelligence - breaking siloed solutions and 'opening' closed ecosystems. This is done by creating a 0FLW dataspace. The ZeroW Dataspace is a comprehensive solution for secure data management, enabling seamless integration and collaboration across various platforms.
